Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026  ·  Effective: May 30, 2026

VettoChat ("we," "us," or "our") operates the VettoChat platform, a lead qualification and CRM service for home service contractors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

Account and billing information

When you create an account we collect your name, email address, business name, and password. If you subscribe to a paid plan, billing details (card number, billing address) are collected and processed by our payment processor, Stripe. We never store your full card number.

Lead data you process through the widget

When visitors interact with your VettoChat widget, we collect the information those visitors provide — such as their name, phone number, service type, and budget. This data belongs to you (the account holder) and is stored on your behalf so it can appear in your CRM dashboard and be delivered to you via SMS.

Usage and technical data

We collect standard log data including IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and feature usage. This is used to operate and improve the service.

Cookies and tracking

We use cookies for authentication (keeping you logged in), preferences, and aggregate analytics. We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. See Section 6 for details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the VettoChat platform
  • To deliver lead summaries to you via SMS and display them in your dashboard
  • To process payments and send billing receipts
  • To send product updates, trial reminders, and support communications (you may opt out of marketing emails at any time)
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your data or your leads' data to third parties, ever.

3. Third-Party Services

We use a limited set of trusted third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Twilio — SMS delivery for lead alerts

Each of these providers has their own privacy policy and is bound by data processing agreements with us. We do not share data with any other third parties except as required by law.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Lead data stored in your CRM dashboard is retained until you delete it or close your account. If you cancel your account, we delete your data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or financial compliance purposes (e.g., billing records).

5. Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Data portability (receive a copy of your data in a machine-readable format)
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@vettochat.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies

We use three categories of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — required for the platform to function (login sessions, security tokens). These cannot be disabled.
  • Preference cookies — remember your settings such as billing plan selection.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how the product is used in aggregate. You can decline these via the cookie banner without affecting platform functionality.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption in transit, encrypted storage at rest, and access controls. No system is 100% secure, but we take data protection seriously and will notify affected users promptly in the event of a breach.

8. Children's Privacy

VettoChat is a business tool intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has submitted information to us, contact us and we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email or via an in-app notice at least 7 days before the changes take effect. Continued use of the service after that date constitutes acceptance.
