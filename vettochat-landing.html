Features
What it does.

Lead Scoring

Every conversation is scored automatically — Hot, Warm, or Cold — based on the visitor's service type, urgency, and budget. You always know who to call first.

SMS Alerts

When a qualified lead finishes the chat, you get a text. Name, what they need, their budget, and their phone number. Every lead is also logged in your built-in CRM, where you can track status, add notes, and manage your pipeline.

Managed Setup

We configure your qualification questions, scoring rules, and bot behavior before you go live. You paste one line of code. Everything else is handled.
